    Problem 1. Create \(\mathcal{H}\).

    Pick a random \(3 \times 2\) matrix \(A\text{.}\) Choose entries not all positive, avoid too many zeros, and do not choose an \(A\) that is already in REF.

    Problem 3. Show \(\mathcal{H}\) is closed under vector addition.
